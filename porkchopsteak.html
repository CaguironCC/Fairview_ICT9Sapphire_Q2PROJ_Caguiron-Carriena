Pork Steak Recipe

This Pork Steak recipe is a Filipino classic made special with Knorr Pork Cubes. The marinade is a simple blend of calamansi juice, soy sauce, and spices that tenderizes the pork and brings out its natural flavors.

Source

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg pork chops
  • 5 pcs calamansi, juiced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 pc Knorr Pork Cube
  • 1 cup water
  • Pinch of ground pepper
  • 1 pc white onion, sliced into rings
  • Optional: Serve with steamed or sautéed kale, spinach, or broccoli rabe on the side

Instructions

  1. Marinate: Combine calamansi juice and soy sauce. Soak the pork chops for at least 10-15 minutes.
  2. Sauté: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the garlic until slightly brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. Cook Pork: Remove pork chops from the marinade and pan-fry. Add the marinade liquid, water, and Knorr Pork Cube. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  4. Season and Simmer: Add pepper and cook over low heat until the pork chops are tender.
  5. Add Onion: Stir in onion rings and cook until softened.
  6. Serve: Serve the Pork Steak hot with a side of rice and your favorite vegetables.
